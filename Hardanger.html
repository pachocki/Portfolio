Overview.

Hardanger Website was created for the task assigned to me by kodehode. My job was to create a design and write it from scratch, I enjoyed writing this page because I love the hardanger area.

Chalange.

The biggest challenge was to create a design that is interesting and at the same time forces you to interact.

Results.

In this project, I used the colors that remind me of this region, which is surrounded by many waterfalls, lakes and a fjord. That's why I opted for blue and orange. In the photos I used the parallax effect thanks to which we can see the view like a panorama. The font I used is a giroy serif.
