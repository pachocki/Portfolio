Overview.

I create this website for my good friend Martin Larsen which is personal trainer. Martin has been working as a personal trainer for over 7 years and has never had a website. Which was a big mistake in an age where social media and the internet are very important. Therefore, in this project, I focused not only on the website but also on social media. Which was a very good step after analyzing the local market.

Chalange.

The biggest challenge was to present the wide offer that Martin has. Larsen is not only a personal trainer, but also a physiotherapist and nutritionist. That is why I tried to introduce the coach from all sides, as the person and good specialist. In a way that does not bore the potential recipient

Results.

In the website design, I chose a mixture of simplicity and modernity. I used bold colors that reflect my client personality and what he do everyday. For the sake of balance, I use a very simple, legible font and iconography . The website is intended as a business card. That's why everything is on one page. Therefore, to give some dynamism, I used animations.
